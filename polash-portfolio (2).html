FAQ

Your Questions, Answered Honestly

No sales fluff. Just straight answers to what you actually want to know.

What's the minimum budget I need to get started? ▼
For Meta Ads, I recommend a minimum ad spend of $500/month. For Google Ads, $700/month gives us enough data to optimize. TikTok can work from $300/month. My management fee is separate and depends on the scope. Book a call and I'll give you a transparent breakdown for your specific goals.
How fast will I see results? ▼
Most clients see initial traction within 7–14 days. Meaningful ROAS improvement typically happens in weeks 3–6 as we gather data, test, and optimize. I always set realistic expectations upfront — no false promises, no hype. The goal is sustainable growth, not a one-week spike.
Can you guarantee results? ▼
No ethical marketer can guarantee specific numbers — ad platforms are dynamic and results depend on many factors including your product, market, and offer. What I can guarantee is: a proven strategy, rigorous testing, full transparency, and relentless optimization. My track record speaks for itself.
Do you work with international clients? ▼
Absolutely. I work with clients across the USA, UK, Australia, Canada, Germany, India, UAE, and more. All communication is in English. I'm available via WhatsApp, Telegram, and email across time zones. Remote collaboration is seamless and I provide detailed weekly reports so you always know what's happening.
How is Polash Digital different from other agencies? ▼
You work directly with me — not a junior account manager or outsourced team. I'm deeply invested in your results, not just your monthly retainer. I combine performance marketing expertise with conversion copywriting and funnel strategy — most agencies only offer one of these. And I'm radically transparent: you see everything, always.
What happens after I contact you? ▼
I'll respond within 2 hours. We schedule a free 30-minute strategy call where I learn about your business, goals, and current situation. I'll then provide a custom growth roadmap — no strings attached. If we're a good fit, we move forward. If not, you still leave with valuable insights you can act on immediately.
